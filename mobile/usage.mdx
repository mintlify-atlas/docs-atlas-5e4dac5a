## Viewing Assigned Orders

### Orders List Screen

When you open the app, you'll see a list of service orders assigned to you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login">
    Enter your username and password, then tap "Login"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Orders">
    After login, you'll see your assigned orders list:
    
    - Order number (NUMERO_DE_LA_ORDEN)
    - Customer name
    - Address
    - Status (Pending/Completed)
    - Debt amount
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Refresh">
    Pull down on the list to refresh and sync with server
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter/Search">
    Use search or filter options to find specific orders
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order Status Indicators

- **Pending** (Yellow/Orange): Not yet visited
- **Completed** (Green): Visit submitted
- **Synced** (Blue checkmark): Data uploaded to server
- **Offline** (Gray): Waiting for internet to sync

## Completing a Field Visit

### Step-by-Step Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an Order">
    From the orders list, tap on the service order you want to visit
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Customer Information">
    Check the order details:
    - Customer name and ID
    - Service address
    - Debt amount
    - Product type
    - Previous visit history (if any)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Location">
    Use the address or GPS coordinates to navigate to the service location
    
    <Info>
    Some versions of the app may have integrated map navigation.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Perform Site Visit">
    At the location:
    - Verify address
    - Contact customer if possible
    - Inspect meter/service
    - Note property condition
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out Visit Form">
    Complete the visit form with required information (see next section)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Take Photos">
    Capture 1-2 photos as evidence:
    - Meter reading
    - Property exterior
    - Customer interaction proof
    - Service equipment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap "Submit" or "Guardar" to save the visit data
    
    If online: Data syncs immediately
    If offline: Data queued for sync when online
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Visit Form Fields

The visit form collects comprehensive data:

### Required Fields

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visit Outcome">
    **Resultado de gestión** (Visit Result)
    
    Select from:
    - Contacto exitoso (Successful contact)
    - No contacto (No contact)
    - Predio cerrado (Property closed)
    - Dirección incorrecta (Wrong address)
    - Cliente ausente (Customer absent)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Service Status">
    **Estado del servicio** (Service Status)
    
    Options:
    - Activo (Active)
    - Suspendido (Suspended)
    - Cortado (Cut off)
    - Reconectado (Reconnected)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Visit Date">
    **Fecha de gestión** (Visit Date)
    
    Automatically set to current date, can be adjusted if needed
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Payment Information

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Payment Status">
    **Causal de pago** (Payment Reason)
    
    If customer made or promised payment:
    - Pago total (Full payment)
    - Pago parcial (Partial payment)
    - Acuerdo de pago (Payment agreement)
    - Promesa de pago (Payment promise)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Non-Payment">
    **Causal de no pago** (Non-payment Reason)
    
    If no payment:
    - Sin recursos (No resources)
    - Desacuerdo con deuda (Disputes debt)
    - Desempleo (Unemployment)
    - Servicio deficiente (Poor service)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Receipt Data">
    **Recibo y Valor** (Receipt and Amount)
    
    If payment received:
    - Receipt number
    - Payment amount
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Property Information

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Housing Type">
    **Tipo de vivienda** (Housing Type)
    
    - Casa (House)
    - Apartamento (Apartment)
    - Local comercial (Commercial)
    - Lote (Lot)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Property Use">
    **Uso del predio** (Property Use)
    
    - Residencial (Residential)
    - Comercial (Commercial)
    - Industrial (Industrial)
    - Mixto (Mixed)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Resident Type">
    **Tipo de habitante** (Resident Type)
    
    - Propietario (Owner)
    - Arrendatario (Tenant)
    - Familiar (Family member)
    - Desocupado (Vacant)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Additional Data

- **Contact Person**: Name of person contacted
- **Phone Number**: Contact phone (if obtained)
- **Meter Reading**: Current meter reading
- **Observations**: Free text notes about the visit
- **Number of Residents**: People living at property

<Note>
All form fields adapt based on previous selections. Not all fields may be required for every visit type.
</Note>

## Taking Photos

### Photo Capture Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Camera Button">
    In the visit form, tap the camera icon or "Take Photo" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position Camera">
    Frame your shot:
    - Ensure good lighting
    - Meter/service equipment clearly visible
    - No sensitive personal information in frame
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Capture Photo">
    Tap the capture button
    
    You can take up to 2 photos per visit
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Photo">
    Check the photo quality:
    - Clear and in focus
    - Relevant information visible
    - Proper orientation
    
    Retake if needed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Accept the photo to attach it to the visit
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Photo Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lighting" icon="sun">
    Take photos in good lighting conditions, avoid dark or shadowy areas
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Focus" icon="bullseye">
    Ensure meter readings and key details are in focus and readable
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Relevance" icon="check-circle">
    Capture evidence relevant to the visit (meter, property, service)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Privacy" icon="user-lock">
    Avoid capturing faces, personal documents, or private information
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Photo Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, JPEG
- **Size**: Maximum 10MB per photo
- **Quantity**: 1-2 photos per visit
- **Orientation**: Portrait or landscape (auto-detected)

<Warning>
Photos larger than 10MB will be rejected. Most modern phones produce acceptable file sizes, but avoid using maximum resolution settings.
</Warning>

## GPS Location

The app automatically captures your GPS coordinates:

### How GPS Works

1. **Permission**: Ensure location permission is granted
2. **GPS Signal**: App acquires GPS when you open an order
3. **Accuracy**: Waits for reasonable accuracy (usually less than 50 meters)
4. **Capture**: Coordinates saved with visit submission
5. **Display**: Latitude/longitude shown in form

### GPS Indicators

- **Green**: Good GPS signal (< 20m accuracy)
- **Yellow**: Fair GPS signal (20-50m accuracy)
- **Red**: Poor GPS signal (> 50m accuracy)
- **Gray**: No GPS signal

<Info>
GPS works best outdoors with clear sky view. Inside buildings or in dense urban areas, accuracy may be reduced.
</Info>

## Offline Mode

The app functions without internet:

### Offline Capabilities

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="View Orders">
    All assigned orders are cached locally and viewable offline
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Fill Forms">
    Complete visit forms without internet connection
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Take Photos">
    Capture photos offline (stored locally)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Queue Submissions">
    Submissions are queued and will sync when online
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Syncing Data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Internet">
    When you have WiFi or mobile data connection
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Sync">
    App automatically detects connection and starts syncing
    
    Or manually sync by pulling down on orders list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Queue">
    Queued submissions upload in order:
    1. Visit data
    2. Photos
    3. Status updates
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Sync">
    Check for sync confirmation:
    - Green checkmark on synced orders
    - "Synced" status indicator
    - Removal from queue count
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Important**: Sync regularly to avoid data loss. Don't uninstall the app with pending submissions!
</Warning>

## Managing Your Work

### Daily Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Morning: Sync">
    Start your day by syncing:
    - Connect to WiFi
    - Open app and pull to refresh
    - Download new assignments
    - Upload previous day's work
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Field Work">
    Throughout the day:
    - Work through your orders list
    - Complete visits and forms
    - Take required photos
    - App queues submissions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="End of Day: Sync">
    Before going offline:
    - Find WiFi or good data connection
    - Let app sync all submissions
    - Verify all orders show as synced
    - Check for new assignments
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tracking Progress

- **Total Orders**: View total assignments
- **Pending**: Orders not yet visited
- **Completed**: Visits submitted
- **Synced**: Data uploaded to server
- **Queue**: Waiting to upload

## Common Operations

### Searching for an Order

1. Tap search icon on orders list
2. Enter:
   - Order number
   - Customer name
   - Address
3. Results filter as you type

### Filtering Orders

Filter by:
- **Status**: Pending, Completed, All
- **Date**: Today, This Week, All Time
- **Sync Status**: Synced, Pending Upload

### Viewing Visit History

For orders with previous visits:
1. Open the order
2. Tap "History" or "Historial"
3. View previous visit dates and results

### Editing a Submission

<Warning>
Once synced, submissions cannot be edited from mobile app. Contact your leader for corrections.
</Warning>

Before sync:
1. Find the order in your list
2. Tap to open
3. Modify form fields
4. Re-submit

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="GPS not working">
  **Solutions:**
  
  1. Enable location services:
     - Settings → Location → ON
  2. Grant permission to app:
     - Settings → Apps → Movilidad → Permissions → Location → Allow
  3. Move outdoors for better signal
  4. Restart the app
  5. Restart your device
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Photos won't upload">
  **Check:**
  
  1. Photo file size (must be < 10MB)
  2. Photo format (JPG, PNG, JPEG only)
  3. Internet connection
  4. Storage permission granted
  5. Sufficient storage space on device
  
  **Try:**
  
  - Retake photo at lower resolution
  - Connect to WiFi for upload
  - Clear app cache
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Submissions stuck in queue">
  **Solutions:**
  
  1. Check internet connection
  2. Force sync:
     - Pull down on orders list
  3. Check server status (ask leader)
  4. Verify login session is active
  5. Logout and login again
  6. As last resort, contact support
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't see new assignments">
  **Try:**
  
  1. Pull to refresh on orders list
  2. Logout and login again
  3. Check with leader that orders are assigned
  4. Verify internet connection
  5. Clear app cache and reload
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="App running slowly">
  **Improve performance:**
  
  1. Close other apps
  2. Restart device
  3. Clear app cache:
     - Settings → Apps → Movilidad → Storage → Clear Cache (NOT Clear Data)
  4. Delete old photos from device
  5. Update to latest APK version
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Form won't submit">
  **Check:**
  
  1. All required fields completed
  2. Date format correct
  3. No invalid characters in text fields
  4. GPS coordinates captured
  5. Internet connection
  
  **Error messages:**
  
  Read error message carefully - it usually indicates which field has an issue.
</Accordion>

## Data Management

### What's Stored Locally

- Assigned orders (full details)
- Pending submissions (queued)
- Cached photos (until uploaded)
- Login token (for session)
- App preferences

### What's NOT Stored

- Your password (only token)
- Other managers' data
- Completed and synced submissions
- Server-side reports

### Clearing Data

<Warning>
Only clear app data if instructed by support. This will delete all pending submissions!
</Warning>

1. Sync all pending work first
2. Settings → Apps → Movilidad → Storage
3. Clear Cache (safe) OR Clear Data (deletes everything)
