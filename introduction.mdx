## Welcome to Movilidad

Movilidad is a powerful SaaS application designed for utility service management, specifically focused on debt collection and field operations management. Built with Meteor.js and React, it provides a complete solution for managing utility service records, field workers, and collection workflows.

<Info>
Movilidad is developed by Discol and is currently used for managing utility service operations across multiple projects.
</Info>

## What is Movilidad?

Movilidad is a full-stack application that connects:

- **Administrative users** who assign and monitor field operations
- **Team leaders** who manage field workers and upload service records
- **Field managers** (gestores) who perform on-site visits and update records via mobile app

## Key Features

### User Role Management

Movilidad supports three distinct user roles:

- **Admin**: Full system access, user creation, and cross-project reporting
- **Leader**: Project-specific access, record assignment, and team management
- **Management** (Gestor): Mobile app access for field operations

### Record Management

The platform manages utility service records with comprehensive data including:

- Customer information and service details
- Debt and payment status
- Field visit results and photos
- Geolocation data
- Service history and refinancing information

### Mobile Integration

Field workers use a dedicated Android app (`movilidapp.apk`) to:

- View assigned service orders
- Update visit results
- Upload photos as evidence
- Record geolocation data
- Work offline and sync when connected

## Technology Stack

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Backend">
    - **Meteor.js 3.0.1**: Full-stack JavaScript platform
    - **MongoDB**: Database for records, users, and documents

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Use Cases

Movilidad is designed for utility companies that need to:

1. **Manage field operations** for debt collection
2. **Track service orders** across multiple territories
3. **Coordinate teams** of field workers
4. **Generate reports** on collection performance
5. **Maintain evidence** of field visits with photos and geolocation
